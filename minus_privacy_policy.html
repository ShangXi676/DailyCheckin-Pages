T-MINUS · Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

T-MINUS is a desktop countdown widget app. We take your privacy extremely seriously. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. T-MINUS has no servers. All data is stored solely on your device and in your iCloud account. We cannot access your countdown events, settings, or any other content.

iCloud Sync

When you enable iCloud sync, countdown data syncs between your devices via Apple CloudKit. Data is stored in your private iCloud database and counts toward your iCloud storage quota. We cannot access this data.

In-App Purchases

Pro purchase transactions are processed by the Apple App Store and RevenueCat. We do not receive your payment information. Receipt validation is performed on-device and does not pass through third-party servers.

Third-Party Services

Aside from RevenueCat (purchase verification), this app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
